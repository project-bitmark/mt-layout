
### Simple Sharing API

* query | data param | default
* `url` | `data-url` | `rel=canonical`
* `destination` | `data-destination` | `rel=me`
* `title` | `data-title` | `<title>`
* `lang` | `data-lang` | `<html lang=x>`
* `canonical` | `data-canonical` | `$url`
* `tags` | `data-tags` | `none`
* `size` | `data-size` | `none`
